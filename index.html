Drop Point Calculator

Building upon the success of the Field Out Tool I wrote this simple desktop application

When engineers are scheduled to attend customer sites parts are often required to fix the instruments. It is much cheaper to send parts to strategic Third Party drop points than directly to site although if the engineer has to travel a significant distance out of their way then this saving is negated.

The application simply determines the closest drop points to site if the engineer is unknown or, alternatively, should the engineer be known all drop points on route are assessed and the drop point that is most convenient is chosen.

Languages & APIs: VB.NET, Google Geocoding API, Google Maps Distance Matrix API

Scope: Several daily users for 100+ daily engineer visits
